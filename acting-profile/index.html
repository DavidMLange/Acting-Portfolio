Welcome

I am a Winnipeg based actor with a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Winnipeg, double majoring in Theatre and Film, and Classics. I enjoy performing both on stage and in front of the camera, and have been active in the industry over this last year working on set for Farpoint Films and Refuge 31. My most recent theatre production was at 2022's Winnipeg Fringe Festival where I played Lord Capulet in Indifferently Reformed's revival of Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet. Have a look at my "Projects" to see all of my current and previous productions.

Currently I am in rehearsals for this year's Winnipeg Fringe Festival, once again working with Indifferently Reformed for their adaptation of Shakespeare's The Tempest.

2023 Winnipeg Fringe Festival runs July 19 to 30! Visit Winnipeg Fringe for show listings, tickets, and more info!
